This website is dedicated to encouraging and assisting voter's living primarily in the Eastern Arkansas Region, within the Saint Francis County area to Cross County area. Why Vote?

The United States of America was built on democracy, meaning ballots and votes have been used in every election since our founding. As often as it is said, the truth is, every vote counts. As American citizens it is very important that we exercise our consistitutional right to vote in presidential elections as well as the elections related to our demographics such as judges and local council members.
