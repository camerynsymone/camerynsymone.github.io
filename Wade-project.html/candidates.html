It is important to understand the foundation of both candidates prior to voting regardless of your political party. Each candidate has given their views on a plethora of controversial topics that many citizens use to make their decision when casting their ballot. Here you will find brief information about the Democratic Candidate and the Republican Candidate followed by a direct link to their websites. Ensuring that you understand all points of views will allow you to make a conscious decision on the future of The United States of America. Let's meet the candidates.


Meet Kamala Harris

Kamala Harris is the democratic candidate for the 2024 Presidential Election and she currently serves as the Vice President of the United States by side of Joe Biden, the current President of the United States. She is running alongside of Tim Walz as her vice president pick.

Key Points for Kamala Harris Campaign Include:

  • Build an Opportunity Economy
  • Lower Cost for Families
  • Cut Taxes for Middle Class Families
  • Make Rent More Afforable
  • Strengthen and Bring Down the Cost of Health Care
  • Support American Innovation and Workers

Meet Donald Trump

Donald Trump is the republican candidate for the 2024 Presidential Election. Originally inaugurated in 2017, he is the 45th President of The United States. He is running alongside of JD Vance as his vice president pick. JD Vance is the U.S Senator of Ohio.

Key Points for Donald Trump Campaign Include:

  • End Censorship and Reclaim Free Speech
  • Protect Parents' Rights
  • Reject Globalism and Embrace Patriotism
  • Secure Borders and Reclaim National Sovereignty
  • War on the Drug Cartels
