Purpose Statement

This website is dedicated to encouraging and assisting voters living primarily in the Eastern Arkansas Region, within the Saint Francis County area to Cross County area. Here, you can find more information regarding the candidates, how to register to vote, and where you can vote. Feel free to click on the links above so we can help you with your specified needs!
