Purpose Statement

This website is dedicated to encouraging and assisting voter's living primarily in the Eastern Arkansas Region, within the Saint Francis County area to Cross County area. Here, you can find more information regarding the candidates, how to register to vote as well as where you can vote. Feel free to click on the links above so we can help you with your specified needs!

We hope to be able to assist you with the most current and up to date information regarding the 2024 Presidential Election! Feel free to use our Contact page to be able to call or email your local offices directly for more information.
